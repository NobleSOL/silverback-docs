# DEX Guide

Complete guide to using Silverback DEX features.

---

## Core Features

Silverback DEX provides full decentralized exchange functionality across Base and Keeta networks:

- **Token Swaps** — Trade any supported token pair
- **Liquidity Pools** — Provide liquidity and earn fees
- **Anchor Pools** — Create custom pools with your own fee structure (Keeta)
- **Portfolio Management** — Track and manage your positions

## Fee Structure

### Swap Fees

| Network | Pool Type | Fee | Goes To |
|---------|-----------|-----|---------|
| Base | Classic (V2) | 0.3% | Liquidity Providers |
| Base | Concentrated (V3) | Variable | Liquidity Providers |
| Keeta | AMM Pools | 0.3% | Liquidity Providers |
| Keeta | Anchor Pools | 0.01% - 10% | Pool Creator |

### Gas Fees

- **Base**: Paid in ETH (~$0.01-0.10 per transaction)
- **Keeta**: Paid in KTA (minimal)

## Non-Custodial

Silverback DEX is fully non-custodial. This means:

- You maintain control of your funds at all times
- No registration or KYC required
- Trade directly from your wallet
- Withdraw liquidity whenever you want

Your keys, your coins.
